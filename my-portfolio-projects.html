ASHS Student Mapping Project

In late 2021, I and the officers of the ASHS Programming Varsity (ProgVar) were approached by the school administration with a request to develop an app that would help identify students affected by natural hazards. Thus, I proposed the Student Mapping Project. I set up a database of student locations and developed a web app to be used by the school‚Äôs student-led disaster risk committee. The app can take a list of affected areas and generate a list of students who live in those areas, as well as a map showing their geographic distribution.
